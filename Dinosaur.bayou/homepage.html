Welcome to The Dionsaur Bayou

>

This is a restaurant founded in 2021. We have everything here you could want for an in depth experience from the dinosaurs outside the windows to all of the themed food and services that you can get. We have four different tiers of experiences that you can get. Which all include different things that can make your experience even better. We have dinosaur animatronics that you can take pictures with. we also have staff that dress up as people like John Hammond and Dennis Nedry to have the whole experience come to life. Our restaurant including the food on the menu are all themed after Jurassic park. So come have fun and enjoy a day at The Dinosaur Bayou!
